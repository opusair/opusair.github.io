Welcome to Opus AI Research!

Who are we?

Opus AI Research is the research division of Opus Clip, dedicated to advancing artificial intelligence technologies, particularly in video understanding. Our team has contributed numerous high-impact publications in top-tier conferences and journals, covering areas such as video editing, generative models, and few-shot learning. Additionally, we actively participate in international AI competitions, achieving top rankings in video understanding challenges.
