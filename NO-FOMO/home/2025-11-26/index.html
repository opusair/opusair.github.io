TrendRadar is a lightweight and easily deployable open-source project designed to provide users with personalized news and trend information, helping to combat information overload. It aggregates hot topics from over 11 mainstream platforms such as Zhihu, Douyin, and Weibo. The system offers intelligent push strategies, including daily summaries, current leaderboard tracking, and incremental monitoring, along with precise content filtering using custom keywords and an adjustable hotness algorithm for trend analysis. This tool is ideal for investors, self-media professionals, public relations, and anyone needing efficient market monitoring and trend tracking.
